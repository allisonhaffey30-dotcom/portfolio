About

Quality is the product.

I'm a Quality and Compliance Director with over 20 years of experience in the consumer goods industry, specializing in product safety, regulatory compliance, and global vendor oversight. I've built and led quality programs across a wide range of product categories — from juvenile products and outdoor living to housewares, furniture, and seasonal décor.

My work spans the full product lifecycle: evaluating design hazards before tooling is cut, managing third-party testing through labs like SGS, UL, Intertek, and Bureau Veritas, and ensuring market-ready compliance with CPSC, CPSIA, ASTM, Prop 65, CARB, REACH, and RoHS requirements. I've driven compliance across e-commerce, major retail, and wholesale club channels including Amazon, Costco, and Sam's Club.

A core part of my work has been building and directing audit infrastructure — managing in-country QA teams in China, overseeing factory and social compliance audits across multi-supplier portfolios, and developing the corrective action programs that turn findings into lasting improvements. I set audit protocols, train and direct both internal staff and third-party audit teams, and ensure findings are resolved with rigor.

I bring a buyer's perspective to quality — having been the client on both sides of the testing relationship — which makes me a sharper evaluator, a stronger partner to suppliers, and a more effective advocate for the end consumer.

"Quality isn't a department. It's a discipline that runs through every decision, from product brief to customer unboxing."
